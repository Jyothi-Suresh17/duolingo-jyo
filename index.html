Japanese

Learning Japanese lets you speak to a lot of people. Over 128 million people, to be precise. One of the biggest advantages of learning Japanese is that so many people speak it. While the majority of its speakers live in Japan, the Japanese diaspora can also be found in countries such as Brazil, the United States, China, the Philippines, and Canada.
